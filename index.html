I am a PhD candidate in Information and Communication Engineering at Beihang University, specializing in aerospace intelligent systems and space robotics. My research focuses on developing safe reinforcement learning algorithms for spacecraft collision avoidance under cybersecurity constraints, and deep learning-based vision navigation for space missions.

Previously, I completed my M.Eng in Aerospace at Beihang, where I worked on single-frame optical trail imagery-based angular velocity estimation for spacecraft. My B.Eng in Electrical Engineering (Aviation Telecommunications) was from the Civil Aviation Technology College in Tehran.
